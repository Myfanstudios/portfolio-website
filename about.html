My Journey

My Work History

Military Foundations

My journey in telecommunications began in 1987 when I joined the Australian Army and became part of the Royal Australian Corps of Signals (RASIGS). I trained and served as a Forward Air Controller (FAC), developing rapid-response coordination skills in high-pressure environments. Over a decade in uniform, I built a strong foundation in leadership, resilience, and communication — qualities that continue to shape my work in web development, safety leadership, and project delivery today

Telecom & Safety Leadership

From 1997 to 2021, I led broadband rollout and NBN optimization projects, managing teams and ensuring safety on high-risk worksites like Cross River Rail. I helped integrate technology and safety standards to national infrastructure delivery.

Digital Innovation & Advocacy

In 1998, I helped pioneer one of Australia’s first EFTPOS-enabled internet kiosks. We developed the solution using InfoTouch software and manufactured the kiosks under license — making us the first to deploy this technology nationally. Today, I build full-stack applications and serve as a board member and safety chair for the veteran support charity V360 Australia.

Charity & Service Work

Veteran Advocacy & Emergency Response

My commitment to service didn’t end with the military. I’ve continued supporting community resilience and veteran wellbeing through volunteerism and advocacy across Australia.

Veterans Support & Outreach

In 2015, I co-founded V360 Australia, a grassroots veteran outreach program committed to supporting ex-service members in crisis. Over 7 years, our team helped more than 600 homeless veterans with housing, welfare, and community reintegration. I proudly served as a board member and safety chair until 2022 — driving real impact through compassion, coordination, and action.

Community Resilience

From 2014 to 2022, I volunteered with the Queensland State Emergency Service, responding to natural disasters, storm damage, and search-and-rescue operations. These experiences deepened my dedication to teamwork, public safety, and helping communities in their toughest moments.
